Estradiol Gel is a form bioidentical Estradiol that has been suspended in a transdermal gel so that it can be administered by applying it to the skin and giving it some time to be absorbed in. Estradiol and many other medications have been delivered in this form decades and along with patches it's the safest form of administration.
