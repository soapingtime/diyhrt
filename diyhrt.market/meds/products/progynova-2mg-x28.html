Progynova 2mg x28

Progynova are 2mg Estradiol Valerate pills. They contain bioidentical Estradiol and are suitable for oral, buccal or sublingual administration.

Progynova is produced and sold by Bayer and it can be safely mixed and matched with any other 2mg Estradiol Valerate pill. Estradiol Hemihydrate pills will result in slightly different levels due to a lower atomic weight.
