Relugolix is a GnRH agonist which acts as an anti-androgen or anti-estrogen. Unlike most GnRH agonists which are typically delivered as a large intramuscular injection every 1-3 months, Relugolix can be taken as a regular pill while still having the same effect. Relugolix is a much newer medication only approved for use in the US in 2019, but it passed all safety tests with flying colours and seems to have a similar side effect profile to older GnRH-A's like Triptorelin and Goserelin.

Unlike many other medications on this site, these products are produced by private individuals, not pharmaceutical companies, and thus do not have the associated safety guarantees. While we only list providers with a positive reputation within the DIY community it's important to understand these risks so that you can make an informed decision.

Open Gate Labs's Relugolix pills and can be safely mixed and matched with any other brand of 40mg Relugolix pills.
