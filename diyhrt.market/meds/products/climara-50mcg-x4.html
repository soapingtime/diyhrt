Climara 50mcg x4

Climara is a transdermal patch that provides a slow and steady release of bioidentical Estradiol into the body through the skin. This is the 50mcg dose variant.

These patches should be worn for 7 days.

Climara is the brand name for Bayer's Estradiol patches and can be safely mixed and matched with any other brand of 50mcg Estradiol patches.
