Girlpotion

Girlpotion is a homebrew provider based in Sweden that started in early 2023 providing delivery across the EU. They've gained a positive reputation within the wider trans community and many customers have reported being satisfied with their deliveries.

Payments

Girlpotion only accept payment via Cryptocurrency using Bitcoin.

Postage

All packages are shipped from Northern Europe, the packaging is discreet. Pricing varies based on the specific country and more information can be found here on their website.
