    Payments

    For security reasons payment is accepted in crytocurrency only, but they try to offer a variety of coins and will add more if needed.

    Shipping

    Shipping is done multiple times a week from the Netherlands or Germany, currently as tracked parcels within the EU. Packaging is discreet and they offer the choice of regular and stealth labeling of vials.
