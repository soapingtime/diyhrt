Injection Supplies


Last updated May 2023

Note: All the links for injection needles are for 1 inch needles intended for intramuscular injection. Please consider using 0.5 inch needles if you wish to inject subcutaneously. Please remember to dispose of needles in a sharps box or sturdy plastic container for safety. We recommend 21 to 23 gauge needles for drawing instead of the standard 18g because it reduces vial coring.
